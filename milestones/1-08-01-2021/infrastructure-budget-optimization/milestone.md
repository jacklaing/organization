## Detailed Summary

This milestone allows us to keep our infrastructure on budget and increment the scope of integration with other blockchains for pocket-core 


### Milestone Goals

- Deactivate all testnet cluster blockchains and redirect testnet validators to mainnet cluster blockchains 
- Resize pocket core seeds 
- Allow PNI to integrate more blockchains
